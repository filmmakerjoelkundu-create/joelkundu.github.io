About Me

Cinematographer, Director, and Storyteller

I'm a cinematographer and director based in London, Ontario, with a passion for visual storytelling that blends technical precision with emotional depth. My work explores themes of identity, memory, and human connection through a cinematic lens.

With a background in Occupational Health and Safety Management, I bring a unique perspective to filmmaking—understanding that the safest sets are often the most creative ones. I believe in preparation, precision, and the magic that happens when everything comes together perfectly.

From intimate character studies to sweeping visual narratives, I approach every project with the same commitment to craft and collaboration. My film philosophy is simple: every frame should serve the story, every light should reveal character, and every cut should propel the narrative forward.
